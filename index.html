Loving Care

At Di's Daycare I provide a loving and safe environment for your child. I have always adored children which has driven my desire to become a childminder and create ‘Di’s Daycare’

Child Friendly

My home is full of wonderful experiences for boys and girls of all ages. I have a spacious home with a dedicated play room filled with all sorts of toys for kids of all ages. I am mum to boys, Zach and Oliver, so know what it takes to keep kids amused and entertained.

Lunch and healthy snacks

I cook healthy, nutritious snacks and meals for all children. I strongly believe that children know their own tastes and appetites. I never force food on any kids. They will be offered healthy, wholesome, varied meals, and can decide weather to eat or not. Although no dinner also means no pudding.

Outside play

Outside play is so important for childrens health and social skills. I have a secure garden area, completely fenced, with a double sized jungle gym and slide. I have a number of outside toys appropriate for children of all ages. I love visiting and participating in the local events and activities that Belfast has to offer.

Dusty

The family pet is a lovable cat called Dusty. He lives outside the house during the day time but will play garden games outdoors.

Sleepy Time

I have a dedicated sleep area for little ones who still need a rest during the day.

Location

I live on the Belmont Road. Right beside the primary school. I am within walking distance of all the fantastic local primary schools, minimizing car journeys for school pick-ups.

Working Hours

For your convenience I can accept children from 7am to 6pm Monday - Friday. Contact me to discuss your needs.

About Me

Hi, my name is Diana.

I am married with two beautiful boys. I have always adored children which has driven my desire to become a childminder and create ‘Di’s Daycare’. My aim is to create a ‘home from home’ environment for your child yet ensure they still receive the stimulation and development opportunities that they would in a day nursery. I am a certified rainbow kids yoga teacher so even on wet days when we can’t get out and about I have plenty of fun games and songs to keep your little one entertained.

Having returned to work twice after having both my children I understand how stressful and difficult it can be finding the right childcare provider so please get in touch with any questions/queries or to arrange a visit with your little one. I operate Monday to Friday, opening hours are 7am – 6pm. I currently have spaces for full/part time/after school places from November 2016 (when I will be officially registered with NICMA). .

Diana
